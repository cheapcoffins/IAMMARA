Band / Project Background

Context

Cheap Coffins is a project centered on disciplined arrangement, tension-first songwriting, and a darker melodic palette. I AM MARA expands that approach into a full-length sequence designed as one sustained statement rather than a disconnected singles collection.

The record emphasizes tonal cohesion, vocal restraint, and rhythm architecture intended to translate effectively across both live and editorial contexts. The current private rollout is intended for conversations around strategic release support and media placement.
